About F9 Brands

F9 Brands, Inc. owns and manages companies in the building products, home improvement and home décor markets. To date, the largest of the portfolio companies is Cabinets To Go, the nation’s largest specialty retailer of kitchens, closets and flooring with 85+ stores nationwide. Other companies/brands include Gracious Home, a luxury home goods retailer, Thos Baker, a provider of fine outdoor furniture, and Southwind Building Products, a manufacturer and supplier of flooring to the dealer and commercial markets.
